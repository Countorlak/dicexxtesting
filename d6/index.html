Need Help?

  • Stop - Stops the timer for automatic rolls.
  • Roll - Rolls the die once.
  • Play - Starts the automatic rolls on a 5 second timer.
  • Reset - Sets the running total counter to 0.

You can also click the die at the top to toggle Play and Pause of the timed die rolls.
